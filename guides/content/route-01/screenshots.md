# How to Properly Screenshot a Pokémon's Stats Page

- Ensure both the Pokémon's ingredient list is visible along with the boost and reduction portions of its nature.
- The berry and first ingredient do not need to be visible as they will always be the same for that Pokémon species.
  - For example, Mudkip will always produce Oran berries and have Corn as its first ingredient.
