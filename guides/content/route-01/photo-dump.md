# Your First Photo Dump

Looking to show off your Pokémon box? The purpose of this pin is to save both you and the members here time during this exchange

There are many Pokémon with stats that are **completely nonviable** and not worth the time and energy to be screenshotted for rating. Using the in-game filter feature will help us narrow down what is worth asking about

Firstly, sort your pokemon box by **Pokedex Number**
Next, there are 3 Pokémon specialties: _Berries_, _Ingredients_, and _Skills_. Notable subskills for each specialty are:

- **Berries**: Berry Finding S, Helping Bonus, Helping Speed M, Helping Speed S
- **Ingredients**: Ingredient Finder M, Ingredient Finder S, Helping Bonus, Helping Speed M, Helping Speed S
- **Skills**: Skill Trigger M, Skill Trigger S, Helping Bonus, Helping Speed M, Helping Speed S

## Clear your filter in between each specialty

ie. put Berry filter on > screenshot > clear filters > put Ing filter on > screenshots > clear filters > put Skill filter on > screenshots

Notes:

- Not every Pokémon that you filter will be viable but the filter will cut out Pokémon that will probably never be viable
- Just because a Pokémon is nonviable, **does not mean it needs to be released.** New players should keep Pokémon that are unique until they get a better sense of the game and know what their account needs/doesn't
- Note that the aforementioned subskills listed for each specialty **are not the only viable subskills for said specialty**
  However, Pokémon of said specialty _do_ require at least one of those subskills to be considered viable
- We are unable to filter by nature! So there is a slight concern we're missing Pokémon **with** beneficial natures **but without** any beneficial subskills. If this is a major concern for you, please look through your remaining Pokémon without the filter and screenshot them for rating. Keep in mind, most Pokémon with only a beneficial nature will not be viable
- **Please do not upload videos or links**
