# Free to play and Premium Pass

Free to play is referenced as 'F2P' and Premium pass as 'Premium' in this page. You can check your local pricing of the Premuim Pass in game.

## Daily benefits

- The F2P Bonus Biscuit is worth 3 pips. A Premium Bonus Biscuit is worth 4. This means Premium users can catch any 5 pip mon using their Bonus and a single Poké Biscuit.
- F2P users require an extra Poké Biscuit to befriend 5 pip mons which risks an All full.
- [More on befriending and All Full](./simple-befriending.md).
- F2P users can earn up to 100 Sleep Points in a day. Premium users are granted an extra 100 Sleep Points for their first sleep session in a day, so can earn up to 200 Sleep Points a day.

## Monthly benefits

- All users have access to the Regular Exchange, where Sleep Points are spent.
- Premium users get an extra 1000 Sleep Points at the start of every month, and access to the Premium Exchange alongside the Regular Exchange. The Premium Exchange notably includes a Sub Skill Seed, which F2P users have very limited access to and 10 extra Poké Biscuits.
- See [Exchanges](./placeholder!) for advice on how to spend Sleep Points.
- Premium users also recieve a Good Camp Ticket every month, a powerful week long booster to more than just strength.
- [Link to the item catalogue (doesn't currently exist)](./placeholder!)

## Quarterly benefits

- Premium users who buy pass for three consecutive months are granted a Handy Candy L, Dream Cluster M, and a Sub Skill Seed.

## Misc benefits

- F2P users can view sleep data from up to 3 months ago.
- Premium users can view all sleep data all the way back to when they began to play. If a user does not have Premium, older data isn't deleted but will not be visible.

## Trial bundle

- The trial bundle is available to purchase twice a year, refreshing in February and July, and has consistently been one of the cheapest bundles available, with good value.
- It contains the following: 500 Diamonds, 10 Poke Biscuits, and a Good Camp Ticket.
