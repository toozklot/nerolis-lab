## Tips and Tricks

### Moving your AB to a new island without wasting your Sunday Sleep

- Start sleep tracking on your Go++.
- Then E-zzz ticket to the island you want to transfer your end-of-week AB bonus to.
- **BEFORE MOVING ISLANDS ON MONDAY**, when you wake up in the morning, end your sleep session on the Go++.
  - This action will use the data from when the Go++ started tracking (meaning it remembers the Snorlax Strength and island you were on before you ticketed).

### Leveling up Mons without leaving them in overnight

- This strat requires 2 max main skill E4E Mons.
  - Before you sleep, swap to the team you wish to use overnight and slot in 2 healers.
    - Overnight teams typically consist of berry mons for sneaky snacking.
  - Sync and go to sleep.
  - When you wake up and before you sync your sleep session, collect from every mon **including the 2 healers**. The heal procs (need at least 3) should ideally heal every mon on the team above 100 energy.
  - Swap to a team of mons you want to level and sync your Go++.
  - Finish your sleep session and swap to your day team, you're done!

### Proc Darkrai for free

- Tap on Darkrai in the morning before you sync your sleep session.
- This lets it so that any energy drain that occurs from its skill is completely mitigated by the energy gain from your incoming sleep session

### Misc. Benefits

- Saves your phone battery overnight
- Lets you use your phone while sleep tracking (easier to manipulate your sleep times)
- The Night Cap Pikachu provides extra berries and ingredients (Apple/Cocoa/Honey). Not enough to make any noticeable differences, but it helps.
- If you spin PokeStops in Pokemon Go using the Go++, you get berries when you sync to Pokemon Sleep. Also not enough to make any noticeable differences, but it helps.
