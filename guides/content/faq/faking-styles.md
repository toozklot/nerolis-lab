# Sleep Styles And How To Fake Them (if you want)

1. Download Tea Time (Android only) and make sure it has the permissions it needs (app icon below).
2. Add a Tea Time timer widget to your home screen.
3. Use these settings (Each sleep style needs its own widget):

- Timer set to 2-4 minutes (Dozing), 8-20 minutes (Snoozing) or 1-5 hrs (Slumbering). These times will be dependent on your phone so you will have to play around with the timings the first couple of days.
- Restart Timer: ON
- Ring Time: 3-5 seconds (increase it if your phone doesn't detect movement, can change your phone's vibration intensity too)
- Ring Sound: OFF (unless you like being woken up)
- Vibrate: ON
- Show Increment Buttons: On (will show the +/- buttons on the widget)

4. Before you start your sleep, start the timer for whichever sleep style you want THEN increment the timer using increment buttons so it is 15-30 minutes to make sure you hit Slumbering first (Unnecessary for Slumbering timer since you've set it to 1-5 hours)
5. Put the phone somewhere where it won't move much so you don't accidentally get a different style.
6. Before you end your sleep:

- If you were Dozing, turn off the active timer from the notification and wait 10-15 minutes while your phone remains motionless.

Tips:

- If the surface is too soft, then Sleep might not register the movement from your phone.
- If your phone does not register the vibration from your phone, you can increase your vibration intensity in your phone settings and lengthen the vibration duration in the widget settings
- Some hard surfaces will vibrate loudly and annoyingly, please move it to a different surface accordingly, or place cloth underneath.
