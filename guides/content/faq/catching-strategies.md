# Basic Catching Strategies

# A Basic Guide to Biscuit Usage

## General Rules of Thumb:

- **DO NOT** waste Biscuits on Mons you will not use, **even if they're hungry.**
- **Avoid catching evolved Mons** as you're essentially paying _more_ just to lose out on a free Main Skill Seed!
  - (Evolved Mons require _more pips_ to catch compared to their base stage)
  - (A Mon's Main Skill level increases upon evolution)

## F2P Strategies:

- In event there's a **single** Mon of interest, use your Bonus Biscuit first in case it crits.
  - It is also perfectly OK to use the Bonus Biscuit on a Mon _without catching it_ in order to catch it when it returns on a later date.
- In the event there are **multiple** Mons of interest, consider feeding two Poké Biscuits to one Mon first.
  - If crit: use the Bonus Biscuit on the other Mon.
  - If no crit: use the Bonus Biscuit on the Mon you fed the two Poké Biscuits.

## Great Biscuit Strategies:

- Premium players could consider utilizing the aforementioned F2P strategies with Great Biscuits since they are the same amount of pips (3) as the F2P Bonus Biscuit.
- It is also reasonable to feed hungry 5 pip Mons to guarantee a catch or uncommon/rare non-hungry 5 pip Mons to "speed run" FL.
  - A more conservative strategy would be to reserve Great Biscuits for use on rare/legendary/mythical Mons only.
