## What ingredients should I hunt next?

1. Make a list of the ings that you have (perms or good temps only).

- if you have no good ings at all, consider starting with Apples, Milk, Sausage, and Honey

2. Type `-cooking` in <#1133112483054354593>.
3. Starting on the right of each row and moving left, find the largest recipes that you can reliably make at least with the ings that you have. Ignore recipes that are too large for your current pot size.
4. For each recipe type, make a list of the ings or pairs of ings that will allow you to cook stronger meals.
5. Hunt the ings from the list that most improve your cooking.

- Sometimes you haven't progressed far enough in the game to hunt the ing that most benefits you. You can return to that ing later.
- Prioritize bringing weaker recipe types up to speed or learning to work around them during event weeks. Rerolling for Curries and Salads because your Dessert ings are bad during a good event is a viable option.
